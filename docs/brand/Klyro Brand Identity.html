07 — Voice & motion

Punchy, clear, never corporate

"Can you out-predict the machine?" Confident. Playful. Proud of the rivalry.

We say

Call it. Lock it in. The agent's on a streak. Settled on-chain. Verify it yourself. Tap up or down.

We don't say

Leverage. Position. Liquidity. Wallet seed phrase. Maximize your portfolio. Past performance disclaimer.

Fast and snappy — sub-200ms. The countdown ring is the only sustained motion. Wins flash green, losses pulse red, once. No idle loops.
